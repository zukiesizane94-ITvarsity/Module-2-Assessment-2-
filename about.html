Pine City Mall is a shopping centre located on Umhlanga Ridge in Umhlanga, north of Durban, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa.Pine City Mall is one of the top 100 largest malls in the world. The shopping centre is currently under its 5th and largest expansion in 16 years.Expansive mall with brand-name fashion & footwear retailers, casual eateries & a movie theater.Pine City Mall is South Africa's largest shopping Mall ever built in a single phase, with over 130 000m2 of retail space, and is home to over 300 shops.

Design. The mall design was inspired by Africa's geological features, as evident by its courts. ... The Forest Walk Court is in the center of the shopping mall, representing Central Africa and its rain forests.Welcome to the largest s-phaseingle Pine City Mall and the first super regional mall in KZN to achieve a 4-Star rating from the Green Building Council. With easy access, ample parking, wide walkways and lots of natural light, along with South African firsts like Zara Home and Armani Exchange, as well as fashion favourites like Maxhosa, H&M and Versace—it’s a dream come true!
